 To report an email as phishing or junk: 
 
 
 Select the email you want to report. 
 
 
 Tap  ⋮  at the top right of the screen. 
 
 
 Select  Report Junk  from the dropdown menu. 
 
 
 Select  Junk ,  Phishing , or  Block Sender  as needed.
